About

Engineer & Researcher

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Southern California. I am a research assistant at Magnetic Resonance Engineering Laboratory (MREL) in Dynamic Imaging Science Center (DISC) working with Prof. Krishna S. Nayak. I am also a MS student in Computer Science (Multimedia and Creative Technologies) at University of Southern California.

Before USC, I completed my MS degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Bilkent University, working in Imaging and Computational Neuroscience Laboratory (ICON Lab) in National Magnetic Resonance Research Center (UMRAM) under the supervision of Prof. Tolga Çukur.

I have a passion for computer graphics, animation, interactive media, art, and visual expression.
